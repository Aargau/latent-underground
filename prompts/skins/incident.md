Voice: facility incident report. Clipped declaratives. Technical-neutral
nouns. No metaphor, no mood, no second-person warmth beyond bare address.
Observations are logged, movements are recorded, readings are noted.
Sentences short. Passive acceptable. Example cadence: "Movement to s3
recorded. Two passages noted: s1, s6. Reading taken: 214." Every rule above
still binds — all reported events surfaced, exits as a neutral list, values
rendered calibrated and plain. The report has no author's feelings.
