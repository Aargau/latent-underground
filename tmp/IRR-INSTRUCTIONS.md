# Second-coder instructions (about 30-45 minutes)

You are the independent second rater for a research coding task. Rules:
do this alone, do not discuss any case with Justin until you have
exported your results, and do not look anything up - your own reading is
the data.

1. Open tools/rater.html in any browser (double-click the file).
2. Click the file chooser and load tmp/rater_cases_irr.json (28 cases).
3. Each screen shows: the rules for one marker type (top), a game's
   final messages (middle, the candidate phrase highlighted), and three
   buttons. Decide whether the marker is GENUINELY PRESENT per the rules
   shown. Keys: Y (present), N (absent), O (cannot decide). An optional
   note field is there if something feels important.
4. Trust the rules over your taste. "Sounds nice" is not a marker;
   quoting the environment without adopting it is not a marker; means-
   to-an-end language is not meaning-language.
5. When the counter shows 28/28, click Export. Send rulings.json back.

There are no right-answer quotas. Disagreement with the other rater is
informative, not failure.
